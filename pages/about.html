About spiderSilk

Reimagining Threat Detection for a Safer Digital Future

our foundation

our foundation

Founded in 2019 by Rami El Malak and Mossab Hussein, spiderSilk was created with a bold vision:

To empower nations and organizations to defend their evolving digital footprint through continuous, intelligent, and autonomous security.

At spiderSilk, we believe cybersecurity should be intelligent, continuous, and effortless. Our platform helps organizations worldwide identify, monitor, and protect their digital assets defending against non-coded vulnerabilities, brand risks, source code leaks, third-party weaknesses, and dark web threats.

We are on a mission to transform threat detection from a fragmented, resource-heavy process into a seamless, autonomous experience.

How spiderSilk Helps You

  • Continuous Monitoring

    Always-on scanning and detection across your entire digital ecosystem, no blind spots.

  • AI-Driven Exposure Validation

    Smart triage that separates real threats from background noise saving your team's time and energy.

  • Third-Party Risk Monitoring

    spiderSilk tracks third-party exposures to help you act before risks escalate.

  • Frictionless Integration

    No heavy deployments. spiderSilk runs autonomously and fits seamlessly into your workflows.

A Global Vision, Local Roots

From our hubs in Dubai, Riyadh, and San Francisco, spiderSilk protects organizations worldwide — securing enterprises like Hitachi Vantara, Qualtrics, BioMarin, and MNP.

We’re proud to build cutting-edge cybersecurity technology from the Middle East, contributing to a more resilient digital future for the region and beyond.
